---
This project explores how HIV-1 interacts with MBL-like proteins on commensal bacteria. We are investigating if these interactions facilitate or hinder viral transmission across mucosal surfaces. Using protein-protein interaction assays and molecular docking, we aim to map specific binding sites between the HIV-1 gp120 and microbial lectins.
