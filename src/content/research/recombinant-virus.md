title: "Recombinant Virus Generation"
description: "Generation of recombinant viruses expressing FLAG and HA tags using mega-primer PCR."
order: 2
color: "var(--accent-purple)"
---
Tracking viral particles in real-time requires specific tagging. We have developed a high-efficiency mutagenesis protocol to insert HA/FLAG tags into the variable loops of gp120 without compromising viral fitness. This allows for complex imaging and neutralization assays to see how the virus responds to different immune pressures.
