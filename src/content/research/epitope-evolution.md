---
title: "Crown Epitope Evolution"
description: "Evolution of crown epitopes within the V2 and V3 loops of the HIV-1 envelope glycoprotein."
order: 3
color: "var(--accent-cyan)"
---
The 'crown' of the V3 loop is a major target for neutralizing antibodies. Our lab tracks the genetic drift and selective pressure on these epitopes in diverse clinical cohorts. We utilize bioinformatics tools and structural modeling to predict how these mutations affect antibody binding and viral entry efficiency.
